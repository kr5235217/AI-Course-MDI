Your 30-Day Roadmap

A structured, step-by-step framework to build your AI intuition. We start with conversations and end with complex workflows.

Days 1 - 7
W1

Conversational AI & Research

Master the art of prompt engineering and discover how to talk to large language models to extract precise information and brainstorm ideas.

Days 1-2: ChatGPT (OpenAI)

Account setup, basic prompting frameworks (Role-Task-Format), managing context windows, and iterating on outputs.

Days 3-4: Google Gemini

Google ecosystem integration, utilizing real-time web access, and comparing outputs with ChatGPT.

Day 5: Claude (Anthropic)

Leveraging massive context windows for analyzing long documents (PDFs) and utilizing its natural, nuanced writing style.

Days 6-7: Perplexity AI

Transitioning from standard search engines to AI-driven research. Verifying citations and diving deep into academic/market research.

Days 8 - 14
W2

Writing, Editing & Productivity

Elevate your written communication. Learn to use AI not just to write from scratch, but to edit, refine, and organize your knowledge.

Days 8-9: Grammarly AI & QuillBot

Beyond spellcheck: Contextual tone adjustment, structural rewriting, paraphrasing, and summarizing long-form text.

Days 10-11: Jasper

Introduction to marketing-specific AI. Creating ad copy, blog outlines, and understanding brand voice templates.

Days 12-14: Notion AI

Integrating AI directly into your workspace. Automating meeting notes, generating project summaries, and building an AI second brain.

Days 15 - 21
W3

Visual & Generative Design

Translate words into stunning visuals. Understand the mechanics of diffusion models and how to direct AI to create specific artistic and commercial imagery.

Days 15-16: Canva Magic Studio

Accessible AI design: Magic Eraser, Magic Expand, text-to-image basics, and auto-generating presentation decks.

Days 17-18: DALL-E 3

Generating highly accurate conceptual art via ChatGPT conversations. Understanding prompt adherence and text-in-image generation.

Day 19: Adobe Firefly

Commercial-safe AI art. Generative fill, text effects, and integrating AI safely into professional marketing workflows.

Days 20-21: Midjourney

Navigating Discord interfaces. Advanced prompting techniques (aspect ratios, stylize values) to achieve photorealism and specific artistic styles.

Days 22 - 30
W4

Specialized Workflows & Capstone

Venture into video creation, coding assistance, and enterprise productivity. Finish the month by chaining multiple tools together for a final project.

Days 22-23: Synthesia

Introduction to AI video generation. Creating training videos using digital avatars, script-to-video workflows, and voiceovers.

Days 24-25: GitHub Copilot

AI Pair Programming basics. Even without deep coding knowledge, learn how Copilot predicts code, explains functions, and speeds up development.

Days 26-27: Microsoft Copilot

Enterprise AI. Summarizing long email threads in Outlook, generating slides in PowerPoint, and querying data in Excel using natural language.

Days 28-30: Capstone Project

Workflow Stacking: Use ChatGPT for ideation, Midjourney for assets, Synthesia for a video pitch, and Notion to document the process. Build a complete project from scratch.
